we have best plans for you

Nutrition is important for living a healthy life. Most people neglect their diet due to which they face many day-to-day health problems. To solve nutrition and diet-based issues, we will suggest a customized diet plan and water intake levels with reminders according to BMI for a normal routine diet. Moreover, It will use several defined rules based on age groups, gender, height range, and weight range and calculate the number of calories required according to the rules.

Moreover, it will also deal with periods of pregnancy (first trimester, second trimester, and third trimester). It will also generate customized diet plans for diabetic, arthritic, and heart patients.
